For Entrepreneurs

Register once and submit applications for all necessary approvals from different government departments.

1

Register & Create Profile

Complete a one-time registration with your business details and required documents.

2

Submit Single Application

Fill out one comprehensive application that gets routed to all relevant departments automatically.

3

Upload Required Documents

Securely upload all necessary documents through our encrypted document management system.

4

Track Approval Status

Monitor real-time progress of your application across all departments with notifications at each stage.

For Government Officers

Department-specific login to review, approve, or request changes to applications efficiently.

1

Department-wise Login

Access your department-specific dashboard with secure credentials and role-based permissions.

2

Review Assigned Applications

View applications assigned to your department with all relevant documents and previous department reviews.

3

Approve / Reject / Request Changes

Take action on applications with options to approve, reject, or request additional information from the entrepreneur.

4

Collaborate with Other Departments

Communicate with officers from other departments for inter-departmental coordination when needed.

For Administrators

Manage the entire platform, departments, officers, and monitor system performance and delays.

1

Manage Departments

Add, edit, or deactivate government departments and configure their approval workflows.

2

Assign Officers

Assign government officers to departments and manage their permissions and access levels.

3

Monitor Delays & Performance

Track application processing times, identify bottlenecks, and generate performance reports.

4

System Configuration

Configure system settings, notification templates, SLA parameters, and approval thresholds.
